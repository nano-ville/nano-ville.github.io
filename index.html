WHO WE ARE will inspire you

We are Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) cum Resort

A system that connects the producer and consumers within the food system more closely by allowing the consumer to subscribe to the harvest of a certain farm or group of farms. It is an alternative socioeconomic model of agriculture and food distribution that allows the producer and consumer to share the risks of farming.

WHAT WE DO will allure you

To make a difference in the society by -

  • Making farming profitable
  • Teaching next generations about the rich culture and traditions of rural India
  • Bringing in decent returns to all the individual members involved
